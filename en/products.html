Transformer Substations

KTP–250–2500/6(10)/0,4 U3 (general industrial; own needs)

Complete Transformer Substations (for own needs) with capacity 250…2500 kVA at 6, 10 kV are designed for receiving, converting and distributing three-phase AC electrical energy at 50 Hz.

KTP are used in power supply systems of industrial enterprises in moderate climate regions, for installation inside unheated premises (for U1 design — outdoors).

KTPSN are designed to supply auxiliary needs consumers of nuclear, thermal, hydroelectric power plants and other critical consumers.
KTP are manufactured in earthquake-resistant design up to 9 points on the MKS-64 scale.

Operating Conditions:
  • altitude above sea level no more than 1000 m;
  • atmospheric pressure from 86.6 kPa (650 mmHg) to 106.7 kPa (800 mmHg);
  • ambient temperature from -25°C to +40°C;
  • relative humidity up to 80% at 20°C;
  • surrounding environment is non-explosive, fire-safe, and free of conductive dust, chemically aggressive vapours and gases.

KTP are not intended for operation in conditions of jolts, impacts, strong vibrations, on mobile installations or in mines.
KTP comply with TU U 31.1-20122892-001:2007, DSTU 3399-96 (GOST 14695-7)

KTP includes:
  • high-voltage input device (PVN);
  • power transformer;
  • low-voltage distribution device (RPNN).

PVN is manufactured in the following versions:
  • Dead-end input cabinet type BB-1, where HV cables connect directly to the power transformer terminals. The BB-1 cabinet is attached to the transformer housing.
  • ShVV-2R — cabinet with load switch and PKT fuses;
  • ShVV-2V — cabinet with vacuum circuit breakers.
KTP use three-phase two-winding power transformers of types: TMF; TSZN; TSZGL; TMZ.

RPNN consists of:
  • low-voltage input cabinet — ShNV;
  • outgoing line cabinets — ShNL (number per customer's request);
  • section cabinet - ShNS;
  • busbar duct — for double-row 2KTP;
  • alarm cabinet — per customer's request.

RPNN is manufactured in transport blocks no longer than 4 m.
RPNN cabinets are a metal frame closed on the sides and top with removable metal sheets.

Inside the frame, circuit breakers, busbars, equipment and instruments are mounted. Cabinets are serviced from the front; access to busbars and cable connections is from the rear.

КТПБУ-630-2500/6(10)/0,4 УХЛ1

Block-type complete transformer substations

Voltage 35/0.4 kV type BKTP

Block-type complete transformer substations 35/0.4 kV are designed for receiving, converting and distributing three-phase industrial frequency 50 Hz electrical energy at HV 35 kV and LV 0.4 (6, 10) kV.

Substations are used for power supply to industrial enterprises, urban electrical networks and other facilities.

BKTP-35/0.4 kV is a block structure of complete factory readiness, consisting of the following compartments:
  • distribution device 35 kV (RP-35 kV);
  • power transformer compartment;
  • distribution device (0.4, 6, 10 kV).

BKTP-35 complies with TU U 31.1-20122892-001:2007.

Operating conditions of BKTP-35/0.4 UHL1
Normal substation operation is ensured under the following conditions:
  • installation altitude above sea level no more than 1000 m;
  • ambient air temperature from minus 45°C to plus 40°C;
  • standard wind pressure — per DBN V.1.2.-2;
  • ice region — per DBN V.1.2.-2;
  • snow load — per DBN V.1.2.-2;
  • Module protection degree IP44 per GOST 14254.

Advantages of BKTP-35/0.4 UHL1:
  • minimal installation, commissioning and start-up times due to full factory readiness;
  • enhanced reliability of electrical equipment;
  • elimination of intermediate 6 (10) kV substations;
  • reduced losses on high-voltage lines;
  • possibility of dismantling and relocation in a short timeframe;
  • convenience and guaranteed safety in operation.

Climatic design and placement category per GOST 15150.

DC Control Cabinet

DC operational cabinets are designed to supply 24/48/110/220 V DC to relay protection and automation circuits at power stations, transformer substations, distribution points, and automation, telemechanics and communication facilities.

DC circuit power is supplied from rectifier (charging) devices and from built-in storage batteries.

ShOT provides:
  • normal operation with mains voltage deviations within ±15% of rated value and mains frequency deviations within ±1 Hz;
  • power supply to DC operational output circuits at total load no more than 11.1 A from the first or second AC supply input, with hot-standby mode for charging devices and battery parallel operation when both AC inputs are de-energised. A voltage of 0.82U battery is available at the battery terminals for monitoring control equipment operability at reduced voltage.

Battery operating modes:
  • continuous float charge mode;
  • fast charge mode;
  • discharge mode in DC control circuits.
  • possibility of manual switching of outgoing circuits, and protection against short-circuit and overload currents by circuit breakers, with selectivity with battery connection and charging device circuit breakers.

Measurements:
  • voltage in DC control circuits;
  • load current of each converter;
  • voltage on battery;
  • current in the battery circuit;
  • insulation resistance of DC control circuits;
  • fault signalling via indicating relays;
  • fault of each charging device;
  • signalling via light indicators;
  • voltage presence on AC inputs;
  • emergency state;
  • voltage presence on connections;
  • transmission via "dry" contacts to external signalling circuits of information about main faults of product components and emergency tripping of the feeder circuit breaker forming (+)ER busbars via flashing light device.

Capacitor Units

Type UK

Capacitor Units are designed for reactive power compensation of inductive electrical receivers and for maintaining a constant target power factor (cos φ) in three-phase low-voltage distribution networks up to 0.4 kV, 50 Hz, at industrial enterprises and other facilities. Designed for networks with low levels of harmonic currents.

The unit includes an automatic digital controller, switching and protective devices, capacitors per the number of control steps, an input switching device, and measuring and indicating instruments. The automatic controller manages and stabilises the set power factor and allows manual control of compensation steps from its own panel.
The controller allows pre-programming of required operating parameters (sensitivity, cos φ, response time, daily operating schedule, control law if required, etc.)

Units allow continuous operation at up to 1.1× rated RMS voltage and 1.3× rated RMS current, due to both voltage rise and harmonic currents.

Capacitor units type UK comply with TU U 31.2-20122892-004:2009.

Design:
The capacitor unit is a welded metal cabinet with external doors. The cabinet contains capacitor elements, switching and protective devices, and conducting busbars. The cabinet doors house a microprocessor automatic power controller, light-signalling and control equipment. UK power supply is possible directly from the substation busbars or by cables.
Supply input — from top or bottom.

Mobile containerized power supply units

PVN configuration option:
Mobile containerized power supply units (MKUE) are designed for temporary power supply from primary or backup sources for linear consumers of various civilian and military facilities.
MKUE are finished products of complete factory readiness for electrical engineering applications.

They consist of two process compartments separated by a gas-tight partition.

Fully welded frame structures ensure enhanced reliability and strength throughout the service life. Necessary stiffening structural elements are provided to prevent deformation of the metal structure under loads arising during installation and transportation of the block container.
The base of the frames includes elements for securing to transport platforms.

Key features:
  • full factory readiness, easy installation, minimum additional engineering works;
  • mobility, rapid deployment;
  • parallel operation with stationary electrical grids via cable or overhead connection;
  • high mechanical strength, protection from environmental factors, vandal resistance;
  • safe operation and maintenance by personnel with minimal professional skills;
  • high fire resistance, compartment localisation;
  • dimensions suitable for transportation by general-purpose road vehicles;
  • compact dimensions (20-foot container size: 2370×2591×6096 mm).
