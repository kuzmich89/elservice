IMPLEMENTATION OF THE MOST AMBITIOUS PROJECTS
In October 2019, a contract was signed between LLC "Production Enterprise Electroservice" and the Austrian company "Kronospan" for the design and construction of substation PS 110/10 "RZTA"
On the territory of the former "Rivne Tractor Assembly Plant", which was to ensure reliable power supply to the future particleboard manufacturing plant. In full compliance with the Customer's technical specifications, ELECTROSERVICE carried out all design, approval and permitting works, receiving a positive State Expert Review conclusion. The T0-2 scheme "Line-transformer block with breakers in transformer circuits and a non-automatic bus coupler" with cable connection to the HV network was chosen for the HV switchgear. The following scope of works with equipment supply was carried out during project implementation.
GENERAL CONSTRUCTION WORKS
* Installation of foundations for HV switchgear blocks; * Installation of foundations for power transformers; * Installation of foundations for overhead line supports; * Installation of foundations for complete distribution device buildings; * Building works for structures and transport roads on substation territory, access roads
INSTALLATION WORKS:
* Assembly and installation of HV switchgear portal structures; * Installation of earthing system; * Installation of lightning protection mast; * Installation of 2 power transformers.
ELECTRICAL INSTALLATION WORKS (kV):
* Installation of SF6 circuit breakers; * Installation of disconnector blocks with Svek disconnectors (ABB); * Installation of CT blocks with current transformers IBM 125 (ABB); * Installation of VT blocks with voltage transformers EME 123 (ABB); * Installation of surge arrester blocks REHIM Y 108 (ABB); * Installation of cable end terminations;
ELECTRICAL INSTALLATION WORKS OF THE GENERAL SUBSTATION CONTROL POINT:
* Installation of control building; * Installation of energy metering system (ASKOE); * Installation of auxiliary needs cabinet ShVP; * Installation of relay protection and automation cabinets with microprocessor terminals VET-650 (ABB); * Installation of energy management, control and distribution system (ASKTII) based on ABB SCADA components; * Installation of auxiliary needs cabinet ShVP;
ADDITIONALLY:
Laying of control cables 0.4 kV; Laying of power cables 110 and 10 kV; Testing of power equipment 110 and 10 kV; Testing of power cables 10 and 10 kV; Commissioning of relay protection systems 110 and 10 kV;
